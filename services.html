Theoretical Driving Course (TDC)

The Theoretical Driving Course is required for new drivers who want to understand the basics of driving and road safety.

Course Coverage:
  • Enrollment Fee: ₱ 1,000
  • Traffic rules and regulations
  • Road signs and markings
  • Driving ethics and responsibilities
  • Defensive driving techniques
  • Road safety awareness
Duration:

15 hours training / 2 days (may vary depending on schedule)

Enroll in TDC

Practical Driving Course (PDC)

The Practical Driving Course provides hands-on driving lessons supervised by certified instructors.

Course Coverage:
  • 4 wheels MT/AT - ₱ 4,000
  • Motorcycle - ₱ 1,900
  • Basic vehicle control
  • Proper parking techniques
  • Road maneuvering skills
  • City driving practice
  • Emergency driving procedures
Duration:

2 hours per day total of 8 hours training

Enroll in PDC
